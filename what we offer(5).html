WELCOME TO FAMY NATIONAL INTEGRATED HIGH SCHOOL

Famy National Integrated High School offers a diverse array of strands for senior high school students, catering to varied academic interests and career aspirations. Four strands offered in FNIHS: STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics), Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS), ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management)Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL)

STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) Aims to foster inquiring minds, logical reasoning and collaboration skills. Furthermore, stem introduced an advanced mathematics and scientific theories and concepts, laying a solid groundwork for their future college courses and careers in related fields.

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) Focuses on the liberal arts, fostering students' abilities to think critically, write persuasively, and engage deeply with societal and humanistic issues.

ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management) This strand paves the way for a degree in Business Administration, focusing on financial management, project management, marketing, human resources, modern management trends, and organizational behavior.

Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Includes three practical and industry-relevant strands: Home Economics (HE), Industrial Arts (IA), and Information and Communications Technology (ICT).

Each of these strands prepares students with hands-on skills and knowledge, readying them for immediate employment or further specialized training. The comprehensive offerings ensure that students at Famy National Integrated High School can pursue their passions and prepare effectively for their future careers.
